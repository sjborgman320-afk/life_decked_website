Welcome to Life Decked

Where choices, challenges, and chaos collide - one card at a time.

In this game, life itself becomes a strategic card battle. Build your deck, play your hand, and outmaneuver fate as you navigate the unpredictable balance of relationships, milestones, and moments that matter. Whether you're starting a family, chasing a dream, or just trying to make it through the week, every card counts.

Choose up to two colors to define your moral compass and personal strengths. Will you seek harmony, embrace unpredictability, or strive for clarity and purpose? Every decision shapes your deck — and your destiny.

From friendships and places to traits, tools, events, and turning points, every card you play nudges your story toward triumph… or turmoil. Time and energy fuel your actions, but how you spend them determines the outcome. Use action cards wisely, equip your life with meaningful traits, and don’t forget to tally your project points — that’s how you win.

It’s not just about surviving - it’s about living with intention, strategy, and a little bit of luck.

So shuffle up, draw your hand, and get ready to turn life into your greatest adventure
