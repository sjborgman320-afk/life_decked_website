Game Guide & Mechanics Overview

Deck Contents

  • Up to 2 colors per deck
  • Maximum 60 cards total
  • Up to 3 copies of the same card

Card Rarity

Rarity does not affect gameplay. There are 6 rarities:

  • Common
  • Uncommon
  • Rare
  • Super Rare
  • Epic
  • Legendary

Card Categories

Action

Playable during your turn or opponent’s if indicated.

Instant

Lasts for a single round.

Permanent

Attaches to a card for the duration of gameplay.

Revenue

Used to earn revenue to play other cards.

Product

Physical goods, may require revenue upkeep.

Service

May require a person to provide the service.

Location

Attach persons for added benefits.

HQ

Required to play certain objects.

Remote

Can have positive or negative effects.

Satellite Office

Extension of HQ, accepts objects.

Person

Represents people with traits and titles.

Employee

Needed to accept work titles.

Title

Applies modifiers to employees.

Trait

Permanently attaches to a person with modifiers.

Items

Provide modifiers at locations or to people.

Object

Placed at locations.

Tools

Attach to a person to modify points.

Card Classifications

  • Gold – Positive Moral
  • Blue – Negative Moral
  • Red – Positive Earnings
  • Green – Positive Project Points
  • Purple – Negative Earnings
  • Gray – Negative Project Points

Setup

  • Counter (e.g., D20 dice) for project points
  • Discard pile for cards no longer in play
  • Draw pile for your deck
  • Shuffle deck thoroughly before play

Turn Structure

  • Draw up to 5 cards
  • Maximum 7 cards in hand
  • Play at least one card per turn
  • Spend revenue points to play cards
  • After both players play, tally project points

Example Turn

Player 1:

  • Plays 2 cards
  • Exerts an employee
  • Draws up to 5 cards

Player 2:

  • Draws 1 card
  • Plays 1 card
  • Exerts an employee
  • Count project points
  • Draw up to hand limit
